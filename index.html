What is TEDx?

Bringing the power of ideas to our local community.

In the spirit of **"ideas worth spreading,"** TED has created TEDx: a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience.

Our event, **TEDxVIT**, is fully independent, organized by passionate local volunteers who seek to uncover new ideas and share cutting-edge research relevant to our community. The **'x'** means it's independently organized, but it adheres to TED's core values.

At TEDxVIT, powerful live speakers and curated TED Talk videos combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small, intimate group setting, fostering a genuine exchange of transformative thought.
