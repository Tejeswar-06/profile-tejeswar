The Spirit of TEDx

TEDx is an independently organized event bringing the global movement of **ideas worth spreading** to our local community.

Our event, **TEDxVIT**, is a platform for local innovators, thinkers, and doers to share their unique perspectives. It is a day where live presentations and curated TED Talk videos combine to ignite profound conversations and foster deep connections among the attendees.

The 'x' signifies that this is a fully self-organized event, licensed under the guidance of the main TED conference, ensuring we adhere to the core values of inspiration and intellectual rigor.
